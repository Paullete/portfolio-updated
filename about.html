About Me

I am Paulette Adhiambo, a **communications and environmental professional** with expertise in **strategic communication, project administration, and digital storytelling**. Currently, I work at **UNDP** as a **Communications and Graphics Design Assistant** under the **Capacity for Disaster Reduction Initiative (CADRI)**, where I craft compelling content, develop visual assets, and support global advocacy efforts.

Previously, I was a **Communications and Design Assistant at UNEP**, where I contributed to international environmental campaigns, digital storytelling initiatives, and branding strategies. My experience also includes working as a **Communications Assistant at Likumu Maesa Investments** and serving as a **Volunteer Logistics Coordinator at Homecare International**. My deep-rooted passion for community engagement is reflected in my long-term volunteer work with **Rotary International**, where I have been actively involved in **development projects, event coordination, and outreach programs** since 2016.

Beyond communication, I have a strong background in **Strategic Management and Software Development**. My technical skills include **JavaScript, Python, HTML/CSS, GitHub**, and **Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro)**. I am passionate about **ocean governance, climate adaptation, and sustainable development**, and I thrive at the intersection of **technology, storytelling, and policy impact**.
